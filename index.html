Threat Library

Phishing

Fake emails or messages tricking users into givng out personal information.

Ransomware

Malware that encrypts data and demands payment to restore access.

SQL Injection

Injecting SQL Code into forms to manipulate databases.

Brute Force

Systematically trying all possible combinations of passwords, keys, or credentials to gain unauthorized access to systems, networks, or data

Zero Day

Exploits a previously unknown vulnerability in software or hardware before a patch or fix is developed.

DDoS

Malicious attempt to disrupt online services by overwhelming them with a flood of internet traffic, making them unavailable to legitimate users.
